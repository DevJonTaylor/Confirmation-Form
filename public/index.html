Molly Bears

Welcome to our Molly Bears Family. We are saddened over the loss of each precious child and strive to bring hope and healing.

We create weighted teddy bears for families coping with any form of infant loss. Molly Bears is here to serve as a community of support and comfort. We'll be here for you, we know you'll be here for us too.

To receive your very own personal Molly Bear, fill out the order form when it is opened each month.
